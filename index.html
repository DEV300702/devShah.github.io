My Webiste

A meta description is the description of, or excerpt from, a webpage that appears when that page is listed in search results. Its usually a couple of sentences long, and tells the searcher what they need to know about what the page contains and how relevant it might be to their query. In other words, its a big part of the reason why a user might decide to click or not click on your page in search, and its also the first touchpoint that they have with your brand.
